Wildrex Solutions
Manufacturer & Trader — Animal Rescue Equipment, Fire Rescue Equipment, Forest Safety Products · Maharashtra
Manufacturing & Trading Odoo ERP Rollout Rescue & Safety Products
Business Context The Challenge

Wildrex Solutions operates across two product lines — animal rescue equipment and fire rescue equipment — each with separate procurement cycles, supplier networks, and sales channels. Before the Odoo implementation, inventory was tracked across disconnected spreadsheets, purchase orders were raised manually, and there was no single view of stock availability across product categories.

The sales team was quoting lead times without real-time inventory visibility, causing missed delivery commitments. Finance had no live view of outstanding receivables, and month-end reconciliation took 4–5 working days. As order volumes grew, the manual system was becoming a ceiling on growth — not just an inconvenience.

Case Study 02
Sai Samarth Playing Equipments
Manufacturer & Supplier — Outdoor Playing Equipment, Forest & Park Infrastructure, Gym Equipment · Maharashtra
Manufacturing & Supply Odoo ERP Rollout Parks & Recreation Sector
Business Context The Challenge

Sai Samarth manufactures and supplies playing equipment and forest tools to government bodies, municipalities, and private parks across Maharashtra. Their order cycle involves custom fabrication — each client order often has unique specifications for size, colour, and installation requirements. This made standard off-the-shelf ERP configurations insufficient.

The core problems: production planning was disconnected from sales orders, so the shop floor never had accurate job cards. Procurement raised purchase orders reactively — material shortages were discovered mid-production. And because invoicing was handled manually in Tally, there was no link between job completion, delivery, and billing — revenue leakage was happening but couldn't be quantified.

Odoo Modules Implemented
Manufacturing
Custom job orders, BOM per product variant, shop floor tracking
Sales & CRM
Custom order specs, quotation management, client follow-up pipeline
Purchase
MRP-linked procurement, vendor comparison, approval workflows
Inventory
Raw material tracking, finished goods, installation kit dispatch
Invoicing
Delivery-linked auto-invoicing, replacing manual Tally billing
What I Did
Process Mapping (AS-IS)Spent 2 sessions on the shop floor observing the production flow for playing equipment. Mapped the actual job order cycle — from sales order to fabrication to delivery — and documented where the breaks were.
BOM ConfigurationConfigured Bills of Materials for 18 product variants in Odoo Manufacturing — each with different raw material inputs for steel, powder coating, fasteners, and assembly components.
Custom Order WorkflowDesigned a custom sales order flow in Odoo to capture client-specific specifications (dimensions, installation type, colour) so the shop floor job card was generated directly from the sales order — no manual re-entry.
Tally MigrationWorked with the accounts team to migrate 18 months of open receivables, vendor balances, and GL data from Tally into Odoo Accounting with reconciliation sign-off.
Training & Go-LiveTrained 15 users — production manager, 3 shop floor supervisors, sales team, and accounts. Go-live was phased: Sales + Manufacturing first, then Finance in week 3.
Project delivery phases

BOM Design

Custom Workflow
Sales-to-job-card automation, client specification capture designed
Tally Migration
18 months financial data migrated, reconciled, signed off
Phased Go-Live
Sales + Mfg live in week 1. Finance live in week 3. Zero downtime.
Adoption Check
4-week review — user adoption rate, pending issues, dashboard finalisation

Case Study 03
Vedika
Manufacturer & Supplier — Greenhouse Structures, Shade Net, Green Net, Park Development, Agri Infrastructure · India
Agri & Infrastructure Odoo ERP Rollout Greenhouse & Park Sector
Business Context The Challenge

Vedika operates across three distinct product segments — greenhouse structures and polyhouse solutions, shade net and green net supply, and park development infrastructure. Each segment had different customer types: farmers and agri-businesses for the greenhouse products, government bodies and contractors for park development, and general B2B buyers for netting products.

The fragmentation across segments meant there was no unified view of the business. Each product line tracked its own orders independently. Project-based work like greenhouse installation and park development had no job costing system — the company was billing on estimates without tracking actual material and labour costs against each project. Profitability per project was unknown. Cash flow forecasting was guesswork.

Odoo Modules Implemented
Project & Job Costing
Per-project cost tracking for greenhouse & park installation work
Sales
Multi-segment quotation management, product variant pricing
Inventory
Netting, greenhouse materials, park equipment — unified stock view
Purchase
Project-linked procurement, vendor management, approval chains
Accounting
Project P&L, cash flow reporting, multi-segment revenue tracking
What I Did
Multi-Segment Requirements GatheringRan separate discovery sessions with each business unit head — greenhouse, netting, and park development — to understand the different sales cycles, cost drivers, and reporting needs of each segment.
Job Costing Framework DesignDesigned a project-based job costing structure in Odoo for installation projects — mapping material costs, subcontractor costs, and internal labour to each project so actual vs estimated profitability could be tracked live.
Unified Product CatalogueConsolidated three separate product lists into a single Odoo product catalogue with variant-based pricing — greenhouse models, netting types, and park equipment — with segment-specific pricelists.
Cash Flow DashboardBuilt a Power BI dashboard linked to Odoo data showing project-wise cash inflows, pending receivables by segment, and procurement commitments — giving management a live 30-day cash view for the first time.
Change ManagementThe shift from segment-isolated operations to a unified system required active change management. Ran 3 change workshops with team leads before go-live to address resistance and build ownership.

Case Study 04
AVGE
Manufacturer & Supplier — Power Products, Forest Fire Fighting Equipment, Disaster Rescue Products, Gym & Playing Equipment · India
Multi-Category Manufacturing Odoo ERP Rollout Safety & Fitness Products
Business Context The Challenge

AVGE is a multi-category manufacturer operating across four distinct product lines: power products, forest fire fighting equipment, disaster rescue gear, and gym & playing equipment. The breadth of their catalogue made supply chain management highly complex — different raw material suppliers for each category, different regulatory and quality standards, and customers ranging from government defence bodies to municipal parks.

The primary challenge was inventory and procurement chaos. With four separate product lines operating semi-independently, stock was over-ordered in some categories and out of stock in others simultaneously. Purchase decisions were made on instinct rather than demand signals. Additionally, compliance documentation for forest fire and rescue equipment — government tender requirements — was maintained manually in files, creating risk during audits.

Odoo Modules Implemented
Inventory & Warehousing
Category-wise stock management, reorder rules, multi-location
Purchase & MRP
Demand-driven procurement, supplier management, RFQ workflows
Sales & CRM
Tender quotation pipeline, government client management
Manufacturing
Product-line BOMs, quality checkpoints, batch tracking for rescue gear
Document Management
Compliance document storage linked to product records for tender submissions
What I Did
Cross-Category Requirements AnalysisMapped all four product lines separately before designing a unified configuration. Each category had different procurement lead times, quality control steps, and customer types — all needed to be accommodated within one Odoo instance.
Reorder Rule ConfigurationSet up intelligent reorder rules in Odoo for each product category based on historical consumption data — eliminating the guesswork-based procurement that was causing both overstock and stockout simultaneously.
Tender Pipeline in CRMConfigured a custom CRM pipeline for government tender management — stages from tender receipt through bid preparation, submission, evaluation, and order award — giving the sales team a structured view of all live tenders.
Compliance Document LinkingStructured product records in Odoo to attach compliance certificates, test reports, and quality documentation directly to each product — so tender submission packs could be assembled in minutes, not days.
Sprint-Based DeliveryThis was the most complex project in scope — ran as a 3-sprint Agile delivery over 10 weeks. Each sprint covered 1–2 product line go-lives, allowing testing and stabilisation before onboarding the next category.
Project delivery phases — 3-sprint Agile rollout
Sprint 0
All-category discovery, data audit, configuration architecture design
Sprint 1
Inventory + Purchase live for Power Products & Gym Equipment
Sprint 2
Manufacturing + Rescue & Fire product lines onboarded, BOMs configured
Sprint 3
CRM tender pipeline + document management + Sales module live
UAT & Training
Cross-functional testing, 18 users trained, sign-off by department heads
Full Go-Live
All 4 product lines live. 6-week hypercare support period.
